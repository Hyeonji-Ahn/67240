About Sheraden

Mission

The Sheraden Community Council is a volunteer-led, tax-exempt organization dedicated to improving life in Pittsburgh’s Sheraden neighborhood.

Vision

We value community, inclusion, and respect to all members.

What we do

Our work includes hosting community events, maintaining gardens, and partnering with other groups to address local issues. We invite you to join us at our meetings and events to help us keep Sheraden vibrant and welcoming!

Proud History

Incorporated in 1968, the Sheraden Community Council has a proud history of working to improve the quality of life in our neighborhood. Among our proudest accomplishments, we played a vital role in establishing a Carnegie Library branch and constructing the Sheraden pool. We have also contributed hundreds of hours to improving Sheraden Park, including expanding its picnic pavilion. Today, we carry on this advocacy through the work we do at our monthly, the events we host, and through the devotion of our committees. We invite you to join us and contribute to the unending work of improving the quality of life in the place we call home, Sheraden!

Among the neighborhoods comprising the City of Pittsburgh’s West End, Sheraden is geographically the farthest West and borders the 41-mile-long Chartiers Creek near the Ohio River. Before European settlement, this land served as Native American hunting grounds.  For most of the first 100 years after Allegheny County’s formation in 1788, this land consisted primarily of farms.  In the late 1800s, a railroad was being built in the exact location of Pittsburgh Rapid Transit’s West Busway and Sheraden Station.  William Sheraden owned a farm where the Sheraden Station is located and sold it to the railroad in exchange for their agreement to name it “Sheraden.”  The railroad’s completion led to many new residents and businesses.  Among the newcomers were many immigrants looking for a better life in this country and a serious contender for the Vice Presidency of the United States.  Today, Sheraden is a diverse neighborhood with a proud history of development and accomplishment.  We hope you will join us in writing the next chapter of Sheraden’s story!
